Investment Thesis

We back the builders rewriting financial infrastructure.

The durable crypto companies of the next cycle will not be wrappers. They will be protocol businesses with structural edges, defensible mechanism design, and clear on-chain necessity.

ExpanseFi invests before the category is crowded. Our attention sits on settlement architecture, capital efficiency, security boundaries, and the rails moving institutional demand into crypto-native systems.

We prefer concentrated positions in a small number of companies where product direction, market framing, fundraising, and launch sequencing still benefit from direct work.
